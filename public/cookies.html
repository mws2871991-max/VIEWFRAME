Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Viewframe uses a small number of cookies to keep the tool working and to understand how people use it. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information between page loads.

Cookies we use

CookieTypePurposeDuration
Session Essential Keeps you logged in during your visit Session (deleted when you close the tab)
_ga, _gid Analytics Google Analytics — helps us understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on site). No personal data is shared with Google for advertising. Up to 2 years

Cookies we do not use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Third-party social media tracking pixels
  • Fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking essential cookies may prevent parts of the tool from working. For Google Analytics specifically, you can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Changes

If we start using additional cookies we will update this policy and, where required by UK GDPR, ask for your consent first.
