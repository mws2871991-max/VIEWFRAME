Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Questions: mws2871991@gmail.com

The short version: Your photo is used only to generate your visualisation. It is not stored on our servers, not shared with anyone, and not used for any other purpose. We don't require an account. We collect only what we need to run the service.

Who we are

Viewframe is a window and door visualisation tool built for UK homeowners and installers. The service is operated by the Viewframe team and can be contacted at mws2871991@gmail.com.

Your photo

When you upload a photo of your home:

  • It is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our server
  • It is passed to the AI detection service (Anthropic Claude) to identify windows and doors
  • If you use AI Transform, it is passed to Replicate's image generation service
  • It is not saved to disk on our servers — the image exists only in memory during processing and is discarded immediately afterwards
  • It is not used to train AI models
  • It is not shared with any third parties beyond the AI services listed above

The AI services we use (Anthropic and Replicate) operate under their own privacy policies and do not use your data for training by default.

Information we collect

We collect the minimum needed to operate the service:

  • Usage logs — we log when AI detection is run (timestamp and count of elements detected, no image data) to understand how the service is used
  • Shared layouts — if you use the Share feature, the window/door layout (positions, styles, colours) is saved with a random ID so the link works. No image data is stored in a share
  • Waitlist emails — if you join the installer waitlist, we store your email address to contact you when a place opens

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics scripts.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic Claude — used for AI window detection. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Replicate — used for AI image transformation. Replicate Privacy Policy
  • Render — cloud hosting provider. Render Privacy Policy

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we don't store images or require accounts, there is typically very little data to action. To make a request, email mws2871991@gmail.com.

If you joined the waitlist and want to be removed, email us and we'll delete your entry immediately.

Data retention

  • Photos — not retained (deleted immediately after processing)
  • Shared layouts — retained indefinitely so share links continue to work. Email us to delete a specific share
  • Usage logs — retained for up to 12 months for service improvement
  • Waitlist emails — retained until you unsubscribe or we contact you

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
