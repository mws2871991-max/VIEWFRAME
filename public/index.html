AI photo detection

Claude Vision scans your photo and auto-detects every window and door with precise bounding boxes and confidence scores.

12 window styles

Casement, sash, double, bay, skylight, tilt-turn and more — each rendered with accurate glazing bars, handles, and opening arcs.

6 door types

Single, double, bifold, sliding, French, and patio doors with correct swing arcs, handles, and frame details.

Light analysis

Real-time estimate of natural light gain as you add or resize windows — useful for planning and building regs.

Inside / outside view

Toggle between interior and exterior perspective. Handles, hinges, and arcs all update to match the view correctly.
